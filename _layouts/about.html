Business Background

INDUS Technology, Inc. was formed in 1991 in San Diego, CA. A rapidly growing firm with an excellent reputation as both a prime and subcontractor providing support services in C4I communication systems, engineering services, program management, logistics, Information Technology, and acquisition and financial management support to the Department of the Navy, other DoD agencies and the Federal Government.

INDUS is a self-certified Service Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business (SDVOSB). INDUS is also classified as Service Disabled Veteran Owned Business for the State of California. INDUS qualifies as a small business under NAICS Codes 541330, 561210, 518210, 541511, 541512, 541513, 541519 and under NAICS Codes 541214, 541219 and 541611.

Eric MacGregor

Eric MacGregor is the CEO at INDUS Technology, Inc. His overall responsibilities include the oversight of all company contract functions in addition to program management and business development responsibilities for both the Newport, RI and Norfolk, VA markets.

Prior to joining INDUS, Mr. MacGregor was a Senior Contracts Representative at SAIC and a warranted Contracting Officer with the U.S. Air force. He holds a Bachelor of Science Degree in Business Administration from Wayland Baptist University and a Master of Business Administration Degree from American Graduate University.

James Lasswell

As president and CEO of INDUS Technology, James Lasswell leads a growing Service Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) that provides engineering, technical financial and program management services for government and industry clients.

Mr. Lasswell has worked in the engineering field for over 25 years. He received a BS degree in 1963, then two technical MS degrees, and an MBA. During his careers he spent 20 years in the U. S. Navy, retiring from NAVSEASYSCOM as a Commander and as Program Manager for Vertical Launch ASROC and ASWCS MK 116-Mod 5, 6 & 7. He commanded the USS SAFEGUARD and served on Destroyers and Amphibs.

Will Nevilles

Will Nevilles is the Senior Vice President for C4ISR at INDUS Technology, Inc. with over 45 years of professional military and industry service.

He is responsible for capturing, leading, and managing multi-million dollars of annual revenue operation across several Navy/Marine Corps and Joint Services business areas at east and west coast locations (San Diego, CA; New Orleans, LA; Tampa, FL; Charleston, SC, and Washington, D.C. Prior to joining INDUS, Mr. Nevilles was a Principal and Senior Executive at Booz Allen Hamilton, a Senior Vice President and General Manager at the Titan Corporation, and Vice President and Co-Founder of a Small California Engineering Services Business, Pi Cubed (now ISL, Inc.).
